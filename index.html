How to use it?

Launch google chrome browser, open chrome://flags and enable all experimental WebAssembly features, then re-launch chrome. The FPS depends on your device CPU.

Privacy Statement

Don't worry. The mask detection program runs completely in your browser, and no information will be saved or uploaded to the server. You can even turn off the internet connection after you start using it.

Project Summary

This is a deep learning project. The model used is modified from Yolo-Fastest and trained based on the Pytorch framework, and runs in the browser through NCNN and WebAssembly WebAssembly . All content has been open sourced on My Github .
